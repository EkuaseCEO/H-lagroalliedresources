ABOUT US

Profundis Farms Limited was established with the sole aim of delivering the best farm produce in the industry while providing consumers premium quality agricultural products at affordable prices. Our business operation started on the 29th of May 2006 with 11,000 day old chicks (pullet) and very few personnel. But now we have expanded beyond the livestock production and diversified well into food crop and tree crop production. Recently, we have have started hatching chicks in our newly completed modern hatctery. Our present capacity and capabilities include:

  • Production of thousands of crates of eggs per day.
  • Production of over a hundred Day Old Chicks per day
  • Ruminant fattening and breeding herd (cattle, sheep and goats)
  • Monogatsrics production (rabbit)
  • Heliciculture - Snail farming
  • Hybrid fruits and vegetable production - Cucumber, Water melon, mangoes, avocardo pear, guava
  • Annual crops: Maize

The startegic aim of our business is to impact the society by providing quality, safe and nutritious produce for consumers at affordable prices. Our products are carefully blended, developed and bred to international standard specifications by dedicated experts. We employed the best of the best specialists who are also the leading professionals in their respective fields. We provide deliveries at our main depot in Oshodi and Shomolu giving easy accessibility to our customers in Lagos State. For our various other customers outside Lagos State, we have an easy and flexible distribution system nation wide.

Profundis Farms Celebrates Milestone: Hatching of First Batch of Chicks in Modern Hatchery

  • By Admin
  • Date: October 03 2023

One of Nigeria leading poultry farms, Profundis Farms Ltd has hatched its first batch of chicks in its newly completed modern and state-of-art hatchery. Using world renowned EMKA hatchery equipments, Profundis Farms has once shows it’s commitment to providing quality Day Old Chicks to poultry farmers in the south west region with a capacity of 100,000 eggs per batch, a testament to their dedication to scaling up production.

The inception of Profundis Farms Limited dates back to May 29, 2006, a humble beginning marked by the nurturing of just 11,000 day-old chicks (pullet), backed by a small and dedicated team. The journey has been one of exponential growth and diversification, as the farm has transcended its initial focus on livestock production to encompass the cultivation of food crops and tree crops while also offering trainings. This expansion has been instrumental in establishing Profundis Farms as a multifaceted player in the agricultural landscape..
